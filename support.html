मिठाई Mania

Mithai Mania is here! Join Laddu on her sweet adventure through an ancient Indian village, collecting mithai before time runs out!

Get ready for the ultimate sugar rush in Mithai Mania!

Meet Laddu – a joyful girl with an unmatched love for Indian sweets! From gooey gulab jamuns to crispy jalebis and creamy barfis, she just can’t resist a delicious mithai.

But today’s not just any day in her ancient Indian village... it’s a Mithai Mayhem! Sweets are scattered all around, and time is ticking! Can you help Laddu collect them all before the clock runs out?

Fun Features:

  • Swipe through a vibrant village maze full of mithai magic
  • Collect classic Indian sweets to score big
  • Unlock power-ups like the magical hourglass to gain extra time
  • Explore beautifully designed levels set in a charming, ancient Indian world


Designed for kids and families – easy to play, hard to resist!

More mithai, more madness, more FUN with every level!
